Features

Everything you need to stay in control

Manage every subscription without connecting to your bank or creating an account. Data stays on your device by default.

Spending Overview

Monthly and yearly spend, trends, next-bill insights, and month-over-month comparison.

Subscription Health

Health score with zombie subscription detection, overlap alerts, and savings suggestions.

Renewal Calendar

Calendar view, iCal export, live feed, and optional sync to the iOS Calendar app.

Renewal Reminders

Local notifications 1–30 days before renewals. Free-trial alerts at 7, 3, and 1 day.

Cancel Guides & Presets

Step-by-step cancel guides for 50+ services and 100+ one-tap presets to add subs fast.

Smart Import

Smart Entry (on-device parsing), smart paste for multiple subs, and receipt OCR from photos.

Local by Default

Your data stays on your device. No bank connection. No third-party tracking or analytics.

Widget, Watch & Siri

Home screen widget, Apple Watch complications, and Siri Shortcuts for spend and renewals.

Import & Export

Back up or move your data with JSON and CSV export, smart paste, and receipt scan import.
